Mission Laws

  • Newton's First Law: balanced forces do not change motion.

  • Weight force: W = m x g.
  • Vectors have size and direction.
  • Resultant force is the overall vector.
  • Drag opposes motion; lower Cd usually means less drag.
  • Forces can be contact or non-contact.
  • Stable rocket design balances drag and stability.
